Frequently Asked Questions

Questions we hear
most often

I don’t know much about technology. Will I be able to work with IMTO?
That’s exactly how we work. We don’t expect you to know anything about AI or programming. We explain everything in business terms, propose concrete solutions, and guide you through the entire process. All we need is for you to know your own business—we’ll take care of the rest.
How much does it cost to implement AI or a custom system?
Costs depend on the project’s complexity. Simple automations and AI agents—starting at approx. 30,000 PLN. Integrations and CRM/ERP/WMS systems—typically 50,000–200,000 PLN, spread out in phases so you don’t have to pay everything upfront.

We always start with a free analysis—no obligations, no signing anything. Only after that will you receive a specific quote with a clear ROI. No surprises.
How soon will I see the first results?
We work iteratively—you’ll have the first functional elements within 2–4 weeks. You won’t wait months for results. For simpler automations, results are often visible even before formal implementation. At every stage, you know what we’re doing and what the outcome is.
I have legacy systems—an ERP from 10 years ago, lots of Excel spreadsheets. Can this be integrated?
Yes—and this is one of our most common scenarios. We don’t require you to discard your old systems. We build an integration layer that allows new solutions to work alongside the old ones. Migration, if needed, takes place gradually—without disrupting your business.
Will AI replace my employees?
No—AI takes over repetitive, mechanical tasks that nobody likes to do anyway: data entry, report generation, handling standard inquiries. Your people gain time for work that truly requires their knowledge and experience. The result: greater value from every person on the team, not fewer jobs.
