Your personal data may be disclosed to the following categories of recipients:

  • Formspree Inc. — provider of contact forms (USA). Data transferred based on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) ensuring an adequate level of protection.

We do not sell personal data to any third parties. Data is not transferred to third countries without appropriate legal safeguards.

5. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether we are processing your data and to receive a copy of it (Article 15 of the GDPR).
  • Right to rectification — you may request the correction of inaccurate data or the completion of incomplete data (Article 16 of the GDPR).
  • Right to erasure — you may request the erasure of your data (“right to be forgotten”) when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected (Article 17 of the GDPR).
  • Right to restriction of processing — you may request that processing of your data be restricted in certain cases (Article 18 of the GDPR).
  • Right to data portability — you have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format (Article 20 of the GDPR).
  • Right to object — you may object to data processing based on the controller’s legitimate interests (Article 21 of the GDPR).
  • Right to withdraw consent — if processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of the processing prior to withdrawal.

To exercise the above rights, please contact us at biuro@imtosoftware.pl. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority—the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO), ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warsaw, uodo.gov.pl.

7. Data Security

We use technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. The website uses the HTTPS protocol (SSL/TLS encryption). Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel bound by confidentiality obligations.

In the event of a personal data breach that may pose a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you within 72 hours of detecting the incident, in accordance with Article 34 of the GDPR.

8. Profiling and Automated Decision-Making

We do not use profiling or automated decision-making that could produce legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affect you.

9. Links to external sites

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., LinkedIn). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We recommend that you review their privacy policies before providing them with any data.

We reserve the right to amend this Privacy Policy in the event of changes to the law or the way the website operates. The current version is always available at imtosoftware.pl/polityka-prywatnosci. We will notify you of any significant changes via an announcement on the website.
