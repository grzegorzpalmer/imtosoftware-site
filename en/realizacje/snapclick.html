The Challenge

The client—Snapclick sp. z o.o.—set us an ambitious task: to build an AI tool that would allow stores and brands to create professional product photos without hiring photographers or using a studio. Entering the e-commerce market, where photo quality directly translates to conversions.

The technical challenge was to ensure that the AI did not produce random, unpredictable results—but rather that the user had real control over the style and context by selecting from a ready-made library of inspirations.

Solution

We built Snapclick AI—a web-based SaaS application for generating product photos:

  • Upload a product shot — the user uploads a photo of the product on a white or neutral background.
  • Inspiration library — over 1,000 predefined scenes, styles, and contexts (interiors, lifestyle, minimalism, seasonal, industry-specific). The user selects a style with a single click.
  • 4 creative variations — the system generates four different suggestions in the selected style. The user chooses the best one or combines elements.
  • AI editing — add and remove objects from the generated photo using text prompts. Full control over the final image.
  • Export — ready-to-use files in resolutions suitable for publication on marketplaces, social media, and store websites.

“A product photo that used to take a day in the studio now takes just a few minutes. Photo shoot costs have dropped by over 70%.”

How we did it

The project required deep integration with generative AI models and careful UI/UX design—the tool had to be accessible to people without technical knowledge. We built our own image processing pipeline: from background separation, through prompt engineering, to post-processing of results.

We placed special emphasis on the inspiration library—each style was tested on dozens of different products to ensure consistent quality. We iterated with beta users for 6 weeks before the public launch.

Results

  • A ready-to-use SaaS tool publicly available at snapclick.ai
  • Over 70% reduction in photo shoot costs for users
  • Support for products across multiple categories: cosmetics, electronics, apparel, food
  • Time from packshot to finished photo: under 3 minutes
