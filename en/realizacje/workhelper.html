WorkHelper — organizing the work of
a field team

An Android mobile app for GB Grupa Energia to organize the work of teams spread across multiple locations — with time tracking and export to Excel.

Android
Native mobile app for field workers
Excel
Simple export of working hours without manual re-entry
Multi
support for multiple locations and teams in a single app

Challenge

GB Grupa Energia sp. z o.o. manages teams of field workers spread across multiple locations. Work time was recorded manually—on paper or by phone. Consolidating data from different teams at the end of the month took managers many hours.

The need was simple: a lightweight, intuitive mobile app that any employee could use without training, with data automatically fed into an Excel report.

Solution

We designed and implemented WorkHelper—a native Android app with a focus on ease of use:

  • Location and team structure — the company defined as a tree: locations → teams → employees. Everyone sees only their own context.
  • Time tracking — the employee logs in, selects a task, and logs time with a single tap. No spreadsheets, no paperwork.
  • Manager dashboard — real-time overview of attendance, hours, and tasks for the entire team.
  • Export to Excel — a monthly report automatically generated in a format ready for HR and payroll. No manual rewriting.

Results

  • Elimination of paper attendance sheets and manual time tracking
  • Monthly report generated in minutes instead of hours
  • Managers have a real-time overview of field teams’ work
  • The app was adopted without resistance by employees — the interface was designed for people unfamiliar with technology
