Challenge

Digital Trading LTD manages a network of nearly 300 bathroom showrooms in the UK. The company’s sales representatives visit showrooms, present products, take orders, and collect feedback—all while in the field. Existing tools (spreadsheets, email) could not keep up with the scale of operations.

The specific nature of the industry required a dedicated solution: a standard CRM was too general, and the complexity of the bathroom product range (sizes, variants, availability) exceeded the capabilities of off-the-shelf tools.

Solution

We designed Vitra—a dedicated web application optimized for field work:

  • Showroom database — a comprehensive map of 300 locations with visit history, contacts, notes, and sales relationship status. The sales representative can see what happened at the customer’s location prior to their visit.
  • Product catalog — an interactive catalog with filters, variants, and real-time availability. Product presentation directly from a tablet in the showroom.
  • Order taking — orders created on-site, with automatic transmission to the central system. No paper forms or subsequent rewriting.
  • Visit reports — a brief summary after each visit: what was discussed, what needs were identified, and follow-up actions. Data available to the entire sales department.

Results

  • Sales representatives serve nearly 300 showrooms using a single tool
  • Elimination of paper orders and manual data entry into the central system
  • Management has full visibility into sales activities in the field
  • The system has been operating stably in the UK market since implementation
