Challenge

Manufacturers worldwide have billions of euros frozen every year in surplus inventory. This "dead stock" ties up working capital, takes up warehouse space, and often ends up written off or sold for a fraction of its value — massive losses that hit nearly every manufacturing company.

The problem lies in the process itself: creating thousands of listings by hand simply isn't cost-effective. So companies bundle mixed pallets and sell them wholesale for pennies, or just write off the value. What's missing is a tool that makes selling surplus at the individual-item level profitable — for both the seller and the buyer.

Solution

We're building Deadstocks.ai — an AI-driven B2B platform that connects manufacturers' ERP systems with a global market of buyers:

  • Automatic listings from ERP data — AI reads ERP exports, BOM files, and PDF catalogs, and automatically generates complete product listings with titles, specifications, and photos. Zero manual data entry.
  • Real-time dynamic pricing — a pricing engine analyzes market data and demand signals, intelligently lowering prices on aging stock or raising them for items with clear buyer interest.
  • Smart buyer matching — AI actively searches for buyers for every listing, analyzes their behavior, and sends personalized alerts when a matching item appears. The platform doesn't just wait passively for a sale.
  • Item-level selling — unlike competing platforms, Deadstocks.ai lets sellers list every item individually. Even a niche spare part can find a buyer without being bundled into a pallet.
  • ERP integration via API — ready-made connectors for popular ERP systems, real-time inventory synchronization, and minimal involvement required from the client's IT team.

"Dead stock that used to end up written off or sold for pennies becomes a source of revenue — without needing to bring in an extra team."

Stack and architecture

The platform runs as a SaaS web application deployed on Microsoft Azure (Azure Web Apps). The architecture is built on microservices with a dedicated ERP data processing pipeline, an AI module for generating descriptions and pricing, and a buyer-matching layer based on behavioral analysis.

Project status

The project is live in production — a working environment that validates the product's key assumptions: automatic listing generation from ERP data, dynamic pricing, and buyer matching. Work is underway on the platform's full target version, which will include complete integration with leading ERP systems, an advanced analytics module, and expanded publishing capabilities across B2B marketplaces. The project's marketing site is available at deadstocks.ai.

We're developing it as our own product — IMTO Software is responsible for the whole project: architecture, development, AI integrations, and deployment on the Azure platform.
