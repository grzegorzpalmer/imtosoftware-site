Frequently asked questions

Questions about the START package

Why does it start at €2,500? What exactly do I get?
You get a complete, working automation of one process: analysis, implementation, testing on your data, team training and a month of monitoring after launch. The price is fixed and known before work begins — it only increases if the process turns out to be more complex, and always with your consent.
What if the automation doesn't work for my company?
That's why we start with a test on your real data — before anything goes live, you see how the solution handles your documents and emails. If during the analysis stage we conclude the process isn't suitable for automation, we'll tell you outright — at no cost.
Do I have to replace the systems I already use?
No. The START package is designed to add automation to the tools you already use — Excel, email, an ERP from years ago. That's our most common scenario.
What happens after go-live? Am I on my own?
The first month of monitoring is included in the price. After that you can move to a CARE subscription (from €500/month) or run it yourself — the solution is yours. Most clients choose to automate another process after the first one.
