Frequently asked questions

Questions about custom systems

How much does a custom system cost?
Typically €25,000–80,000 net, spread across stages — you pay for each successive working module, not everything upfront. The first usable stage (pilot) is usually 30–40% of the budget. You get a precise quote with a timeline after the free analysis.
How long does it take to build?
First working version — usually 3–4 months (for a system integrating 150 production machines, it took us 4 months). We develop the full system in stages over 6–12 months, with your company already using finished modules from the first stage.
What about maintenance after go-live?
We stay on as a technology partner through the CARE subscription: monitoring, development, training and a fast response to issues. The code and system are your property — if you decide to switch providers, we don't lock anything down.
I'm worried about a project that never ends.
That's why we work iteratively: every 2 weeks you see new working features, and every stage has a fixed scope and price. If you decide the pilot is enough — we stop there. No penalties, no multi-year lock-in.
