Frequently asked questions

Questions about AI agents

What's the difference between an AI agent and a regular chatbot?
A chatbot answers questions. An AI agent does the work: it reads documents, executes defined steps, enters data into systems, and prepares quotes and reports. A chatbot is just one possible "form" an agent can take — most often we deploy agents that work in the background, inside your processes.
Won't the agent make mistakes?
Every implementation starts with tests on your real data, and we measure accuracy (e.g. in the DocFlow project — 94% document classification confidence). For uncertain cases, the agent doesn't guess — it flags them for a human decision. You decide what the agent can do on its own and what requires approval.
How much does it cost and what does the price depend on?
The typical range is €10,000–25,000 net, depending on the number of processes and integrations with your systems. If you'd rather start with a smaller step — a single process is delivered through the START package from €2,500. The quote is always fixed and preceded by a free analysis.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We work on Microsoft Azure infrastructure with EU processing, we sign a data processing agreement, and your data is never used to train public models. We agree on access and permissions together during the analysis stage.
