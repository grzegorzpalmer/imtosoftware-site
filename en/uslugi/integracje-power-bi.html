Frequently asked questions

Questions about integrations

I have a 10-year-old ERP with no API. Can it be integrated?
In the vast majority of cases — yes. We work with databases, exchange files, and as a last resort, interface automation. Legacy systems are our most common scenario — we don't require you to replace them.
Will the integration bring the business to a halt?
No. We work alongside your live systems and cut over in stages, outside peak hours. Your business runs normally throughout the entire implementation.
How long does a Power BI implementation take?
The first working dashboard usually within 2–3 weeks of getting access to your data. A full implementation with integrations — 4–8 weeks. If the data needs cleanup first, we'll tell you outright during the free analysis.
